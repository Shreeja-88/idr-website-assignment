About IDR

Where Expertise Meets Emerging Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is a forward-thinking centre dedicated to equipping professionals with the knowledge and skills to navigate the evolving landscape of cyber, technology, and digital risk management.

We bridge academia, industry, and government to build a resilient, talent-rich ecosystem for the digital age.
